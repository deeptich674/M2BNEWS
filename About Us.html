About Us

Allow us to introduce to you Our M2B News

m2bnews.tv is the augmentation of M/s Matha’s Media Broadcasting, basically a Print and Electronic Media, carrying out sting operations, publishing and airing Crime & Political News. m2bnews.tv is a news platform for India across the globe, using technology at par with others in the field of Journalism. We all are aware, that today digital media has taken the most crucial position in International Journalism [Print & Electronic Media]. Digital media has always stood as the source of information to the world. Digital media is one such source that can spread any tiny or mighty news to anywhere in the world.

Any Big or Small, Public or Private, National or Multinational establishments depend on the internet for every microscopic information. After Shri Narendra Modi has become Prime minister of India, there is a vast acceptance of digitalization. India has become 2nd largest user of internet in the world, and has immense potential in terms of social media. We at m2bnews.tv have launched our digital and cable channel considering the cultural, social and human values as one of the driving forces.

Today we see most of the news channels are either owned by a particular community promoting their leaders or a particular party using for campaigning activities, rather than delivering the right information to the people. Indian media is mostly influenced by money launderers, business tycoons & some corrupt politicians. In today’s world, media centre’s running on ethical values are facing difficulties to survive in the market and even face closure as well.

m2bnews.tv is omnipresent across the country to cover latest news and bring them to you in the shortest span possible. The advancement of technology in the mobile sector initiates live coverage of the true incidents instantaneously thereby obligating us to say “REFLECTION OF TRUTH”. We at m2bnews.tv strive hard to deliver the latest information from Government, Private, Corporate & Political sectors to every nook & corner of the country through Digital and Cable technology. Be it in any part of the world, m2bnews.tv gives you latest news about your domicile State and Country, thereby uniting 29 states & 9 Union Territories into one News Portal. We also are keenly working towards news casting in all regional languages for first time in India with the help of Team SPARC [Socio Political Analysis Research Council] to make it possible. India is exceptionally booming Scientifically & Technologically, thus surpassing other developing Countries. But Corruption, Politics, Terrorism, Money laundering, White Collar Crimes, Cyber Crimes, Mafia, Fake Spiritual Gurus, Casting Couch, Environmental degradation, Yellow Journalism, Scams & Scandals are causing discomfort and inconvenience for the common man to survive.

Our mission is to use the Power of Word & Voice to Eliminate Corruption. Our Practice is to pen on Corrupt Politics. Our Motto is to extinguish Yellow Journalism from the Media World. Our Aim is to bring Inhuman and Illegal Practices to Limelight.

Now a day’s digital media playing an effective role in the print media, some peopled taking advantage of this and making money with controversial heading with no content. We want to save the valuable timed of public, directly tell the story/subject with single take /without all controversial content within 2-5 mins. Our main objective is to save the valuable resource (time) of the viewers. So, we have started M2B with the above intention. We need all your valuable support to move the public to utilize the time effectively and for the growth of individual growth, ultimately affect the growth of the country.

m2bnews.tv team.
